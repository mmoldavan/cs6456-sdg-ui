To Paddle

A full paddle movement consists of the completion of an up and down stroke, in that order. Tilting the phone up on its left / right axis is an up stroke, and tilting it down is a down stroke as shown:

Paddling has variable speed based on the speed in which you complete the motion.

To Jump

Jump by tilting the phone into a fully vertical position. Your partner must complete the jump motion simultaneously to jump successfully.
